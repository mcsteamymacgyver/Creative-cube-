How Lost and Found Works

It's as simple as 1, 2, 3. No complicated steps.

1

You Report Your Lost Item

Fill in a simple form on this website. Tell us what you lost, where you lost it, and give a few details only you would know.

2

Someone Reports It Found

A kind person who finds your item visits the website and reports that they have found something. We match it with your report.

3

We Verify & Reunite You

Before handing anything over, we ask you a few questions to confirm it is really yours. Then we connect you with the finder.
