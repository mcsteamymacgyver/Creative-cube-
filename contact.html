What Lost & Found Does to Help

These are the practical ways our platform solves the lost & found problem in our community.

An Online Reporting Page

Instead of going from office to office hoping someone turned in your item, you can report it right here anytime, from any phone or computer.

One Central Place for All Reports

All lost and found reports are kept in one place. This makes it easy to compare and match items, so nothing slips through the cracks.

Safe Verification Before Handover

We never just hand an item to anyone who claims it. We always ask questions first to confirm the person is truly the owner.

Community Volunteers as Drop Points

We encourage honest community members to become local drop-off points where found items can be left safely while we track down the owner.

Spreading Awareness

We share news about this platform in schools, markets, and taxi parks and other public places so more people know it exists and can use it when they need it.

Quick Contact When a Match Is Found

The moment we find a match for your report, we reach out by call, SMS, or email so the wait time is as short as possible.
